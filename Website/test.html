    Behind The Lens

    Hi! I'm the photographer behind the lens, the creator of Olive Branch Photography. My name is Sarah Whiting, I'm a mother of two precious little ones, wife to an amazing man, and an artist with a camera in my hand.


    Photography took me by surprise. In high school I took a photography class, pretty basic, with a point and shoot camera. My interest level was low and I felt uninspired. Years later, after marrying the love of my life and the birth of our second child, my husband decided it was time to upgrade our camera. At first I was terrified of our new DSLR, but with time I grew more and more determined to learn it's ins and outs and with that I grew to love photography with a passion I didn't know was in me.


    Today I endeavor to share this passion with you by capturing life happening. Relationships, beautiful vulnerability, joy, love, precious moments that don't last long enough, these are what inspire me. In a single image I desire to freeze a laugh, a breath, a glance of love, a silly face, a jump of joy, a smile of happiness; a treasure for you to keep forever.

    These sessions are 30 minutes long, include 15 edited digital images with printing rights, and are available for Babywearing, Breastfeeding, and Love {Engagement, Anniversary, Couples, Best Friends} photo packages. In home lifestyle mini-sessions are available for Breastfeeding sessions. For all mini-sessions a variety of locations in Northwest Tucson are available.

    Family/Maternity/Newborn

    $120

    Family and Maternity sessions are 60-90 minutes long and include 50 edited digital images with printing rights. A variety of shooting locations in Tucson are available. Family sessions include up to 6 people. Please contact me for information about larger family photo sessions.


    Newborn sessions are 2-3 hours long to accomodate feeding, diaper changes, and the comfort of baby and family. Included are 50 edited digital images with printing rights. These sessions are typically done in home for the comfort of the baby and family and are lifestyle photo shoots.


    Discounted Maternity and Newborn packages are available. Please contact me for more details.
